Why ADUC

The missing layer between data and AI.

Data can be machine-readable and still machine-ambiguous. ADUC turns the interpretation hidden in prompts, code and private mappings into a portable contract.

Readable≠UnderstoodADUC makes the difference explicit.
01 Syntax02 Prompts03 Lock-in04 What changes

01 · Syntax

JSON and CSV carry values, not a shared interpretation.

A field named flow may be a discharge rate, a business process or a boolean state. A number does not identify its quantity kind, unit, timestamp role, authority or uncertainty.

Data alone{"flow": 1.5}multiple plausible meanings

02 · Prompts

A prompt explains one interaction. It is not a portable contract.

Prompt text is difficult to version, validate, compare and audit across models. It may be omitted from the next integration or interpreted differently by another provider.

Prompt“flow means water discharge”workflow-specific and hard to test

03 · Lock-in

Private mappings make every consumer relearn the same source.

ETL jobs and connectors often know the real meaning, but that knowledge stays trapped in proprietary code. The next AI system must rebuild it, trust undocumented assumptions or guess.

04 · What changes

ADUC makes understanding a versioned object.

Structure, semantics, identity, context, provenance, uncertainty, relations and use conditions can travel together and be checked by the same deterministic rules.

BeforePrompts · code · mappings · docsfragmented, implicit, provider-specific
With ADUCOne explicit understanding contractportable, comparable, reviewable

Concrete consequences

Small ambiguities become large operational errors.

01

Two station identifiers

Without ADUC: systems may merge R42 and 897 because labels look similar.

With ADUC: identity remains unknown until qualifying evidence supports equivalence. Correlation does not become identity.

02

Celsius and Fahrenheit

Without ADUC: values can be combined as if their units were identical.

With ADUC: quantity kind, unit identity and conversion evidence make a deterministic conversion possible.

03

Conditions of use

Without ADUC: permission may be assumed from access to the bytes.

With ADUC: policy evaluation can return permit, deny, indeterminate or requiresHumanReview. It still does not grant legal permission by itself.

Composition, not replacement

ADUC connects responsibilities that existing standards already handle well.

It can reference JSON Schema, RDF/OWL, PROV-O, DQV, ODRL, QUDT, UCUM and other established vocabularies. The Core provides one contract boundary for AI consumers.

Quick distinctions

What ADUC is — and is not.

Is ADUC an AI model?

No. It is a machine-readable contract consumed by AI systems, tools and applications.

Is ADUC the same as MCP?

No. MCP transports tool and agent interactions. A future adapter could transport an ADUC contract, but MCP is not the Core.

Is ADUC a universal ontology?

No. It references external vocabularies and ontologies instead of replacing them.

Does ADUC reveal private model reasoning?

No. It makes declared consumer behavior and contract handling auditable; it does not expose private chain-of-thought.
