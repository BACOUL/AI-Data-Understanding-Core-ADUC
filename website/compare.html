Normative assessment vocabulary

Results preserve uncertainty and policy.

equivalentconvertiblecompatibleincompatibleunknowncontesteddeprecatedprohibitedrequiresHumanReview

Safe comparison

No evidence, no invented equivalence.

Units

Convertible only when quantity kind and an allowed conversion are established.

Identity

Matching labels do not merge entities. A qualifying identity decision is required.

Relations

Close match is not equality. Direction, authority and evidence remain explicit.

Policy

Permission is never inferred from data access or schema compatibility.

Example interpretation

The same structural change can lead to different assessments.

unitIrimodifiedconvertible

Known deterministic conversion with matching quantity kind.

identity assertionaddedrequiresHumanReview

Evidence exists but the authority level is insufficient for an automatic merge.

policy prohibitionaddedprohibited

The requested use is explicitly denied.
