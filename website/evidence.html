What evidence does not exist yet

No external two-consumer AI proof has completed.

No absolute first-world claim is made. A credible external claim requires two independent consumers, frozen inputs, controlled with/without-ADUC tests, published reports and reproducible failure analysis.

Future conformance

Conformance classes remain roadmap work.

Schema conformance, complete-Core validation, comparison behavior and consumer-behavior audits may become distinct classes. They are not yet presented as a mature certification program.
