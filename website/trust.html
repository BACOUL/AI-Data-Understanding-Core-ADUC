Trust model

Explicit evidence beats confident guessing.

ADUC is AI-first, not AI-only. It makes unknowns, conflicts, authority and use conditions visible to machines and people.

UNKNOWNis a valid resultno silent promotion
01

Provider-neutral

No model-specific reasoning format is required. Consumers receive the same contract and the same deterministic checks.

02

Human review

Requires-human-review is an explicit outcome, not a failure to hide.

03

Privacy by default

Validation operates locally and does not upload source data or resolve remote schemas without approval.

04

Security by design

Input size, depth, node count and graph behavior are bounded. Duplicate JSON members and dangerous references are rejected.

05

Authority stays separate

Confidence, evidence and authority are distinct. High confidence does not create canonical status.

06

No automatic legal permission

Policy conditions can be evaluated, but ADUC is not legal advice or a production access-control system.

Failure-safe states

What the contract cannot establish remains visible.

unknowninferredcontesteddeprecatedcanonicalrequires human review

Operational limits

Trust requires boundaries.

01 No unapproved remote schema or vocabulary resolution.

02 No inferred publisher authority, identity equivalence or legal permission.

03 No silent repair of invalid complete-Core contracts.

04 No claim that a valid contract proves truth in the world.
