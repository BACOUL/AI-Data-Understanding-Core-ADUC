The Core model

Ten responsibilities, separated on purpose.

The Core is a normative envelope for complete data understanding. Required source binding stays distinct from optional meaning, identity, evidence, uncertainty, relations and policy.

Minimum interoperable envelopeaduc + resource + structure

Optional modules are included when their facts are available and relevant. Missing evidence remains visible rather than being guessed.

01aducrequired

Contract identity

Version, publisher, status and declared profiles.

"coreVersion": "0.1.0-alpha.0"

Boundary: Identifies the contract; it does not certify the truth of every assertion.

02resourcerequired

Original resource

Kind, media type, digest, version and locator.

"digest": "aaaaaaaa…"

Boundary: Binds the source; it does not embed or replace the source bytes.

03structurerequired

Addressable shape

Records, fields, types and source paths.

"sourcePath": "$.flow"

Boundary: Describes addressable shape; it does not define domain meaning.

04semanticsoptional

Declared meaning

Concept IRIs, units, mappings and qualifications.

"conceptIri": "…/WaterDischarge"

Boundary: Declares meaning; it does not create a universal vocabulary.

05identityoptional

Entity decisions

Identifiers, entities and evidence-backed equivalence.

"decision": "exactEntity"

Boundary: Records evidence-backed identity decisions; matching labels are insufficient.

06contextoptional

Interpretation frame

Temporal, spatial and operational conditions.

"timezoneIri": "urn:iana:tz:UTC"

Boundary: Defines interpretation conditions; it does not infer missing operational facts.

07provenanceoptional

Evidence trail

Agents, activities, entities and derivation.

"typeIri": "http://www.w3.org/ns/prov#Activity"

Boundary: Records agents and activities; it does not prove trustworthiness automatically.

08uncertaintyoptional

Limits and quality

Measurement uncertainty, confidence and quality.

"uncertaintyType": "relative"

Boundary: Preserves limits and quality; it does not convert confidence into authority.

09relationsoptional

Typed connections

Dependencies, mappings, derivations and correlations.

"relationIri": "http://www.w3.org/2004/02/skos/core#closeMatch"

Boundary: Declares typed connections; transitivity and causality are never assumed.

10policyoptional

Use conditions

Permit, deny, indeterminate and human-review outcomes.

"effect": "deny"

Boundary: Describes use conditions; it is not legal advice or an access-control engine.

Responsibility boundaries

One fact should have one owner.

01 A field path belongs to structure; its domain meaning belongs to semantics.

02 An entity identifier belongs to identity; the activity that asserted it belongs to provenance.

03 Measurement uncertainty belongs to uncertainty; semantic authority remains a separate qualification.

04 Use conditions belong to policy; access to the bytes does not imply permission.
